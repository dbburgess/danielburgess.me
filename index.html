About

Hey there, I'm Daniel. Let me tell you a little bit about me...I'm an entrepreneur, problem solver, software engineer, web developer, and app creator. Well, there is actually much more to me than that, of course, but it's a good start!

I live in Radford, nestled in the beautiful New River Valley of Virginia. My family moved here as I was approaching the teenage years because my Father got a job in a startup during the dot-com bubble. That didn't end up panning out too well (as was the case for many startups), but I love the area and am glad I ended up here!

I was homeschooled my whole life, and got my first taste of a regular classroom at New River Community College, where I received two associate's degrees. I enrolled at Radford University, where I dove in and earned a bachelor's degree. While at Radford, I had the privilege of being one of the first students involved in the GAMeS Lab, forging the path with innovative research in education using technology!

That research was the catalyst for the spin-off company MoGo Mobile, which has been working to commercialize FreshAiR, an augmented reality platform developed in the GAMeS Lab. As a co-founder, I've worn quite a lot of hats in that pursuit, and learned a lot along the way!

If you have any questions, feel free to contact me. I'm happy to field any questions you may have!
